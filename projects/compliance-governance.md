# Compliance & Information Security Governance Program

## Objective
Design and lead a comprehensive compliance and governance framework
to ensure the organization meets regulatory, security, and business
requirements while operating in a remote-first environment.

## Business Context
The organization handled sensitive customer and business data across
cloud platforms and SaaS applications. A structured governance model
was required to reduce regulatory risk, support audits, and establish
clear accountability across IT and business teams.

## Scope of Governance
- Information Security Management
- Data Protection & Privacy
- Risk Management
- Policy & Standards Management
- Audit & Compliance Readiness

## Regulatory & Framework Alignment
- GDPR (Data Protection & Privacy)
- PCI DSS (Payment Data Security)
- ISO 27001 principles
- Internal security and risk policies

## Key Responsibilities
- Defined and maintained information security policies, standards,
  and procedures
- Led risk assessments and compliance gap analysis
- Partnered with Legal, IT, and Business stakeholders
- Provided executive-level visibility into compliance posture
- Acted as point of contact for audits and assessments

## Governance Controls Implemented
- Data classification and handling standards
- Access control and identity governance policies
- Vendor risk assessment framework
- Incident management and breach response alignment
- Change management and exception handling process

## Audit & Risk Management
- Conducted periodic internal reviews and control testing
- Maintained risk register with remediation tracking
- Coordinated evidence collection for audits
- Managed compliance findings through closure

## Documentation & Reporting
- Security policies and standards repository
- Risk assessment reports
- Compliance dashboards and metrics
- Audit readiness documentation

## Outcomes
- Improved audit readiness and compliance confidence
- Reduced regulatory and operational risk
- Clear ownership and accountability for security controls
- Strong alignment between security, IT, and business objectives
