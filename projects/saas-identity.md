# SaaS & Identity Security (GSuite, Okta, Slack)

## Objective
Secure SaaS platforms while enabling efficient collaboration
for distributed teams.

## Platforms
- GSuite
- Okta
- Slack

## Controls
- SSO & MFA enforcement
- Role-based access
- Quarterly access reviews

## Outcome
- Reduced access risk
- Faster onboarding/offboarding
